Frequently Asked Questions

What logistics and after-sales support do you offer?

We partner with DHL, FedEx and reliable sea freight forwarders to provide door-to-door delivery worldwide, and handle all customs documentation for you. If you receive products that don't match the COA standards, we offer free returns/exchanges and cover all shipping costs.

Do you support custom ingredient specifications or formulation?

Yes! Our in-house R&D team can customize ingredient purity, extraction methods, and formulation blends to match your exact requirements, with free sample testing before mass production.

How do you handle quality control for bulk orders?

Every batch goes through 3 rounds of third-party testing before shipment, and we provide full COA, MSDS and compliance documents with every order.

What's your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

We offer flexible MOQs for all products: 1kg trial orders are available for new customers to test quality, and we can handle bulk orders from 50kg to 10,000kg+ for mass production.

Do you provide samples?

Yes, we can provide 10-50g free samples for quality testing (you only cover shipping cost). Sample fees can be fully deducted from your first bulk order.

What certifications do you provide?

All our partner factories are GMP, ISO 9001, HACCP certified. We provide all required compliance documents including COA, MSDS, FDA registration, REACH declaration, Kosher and Halal certificates for every order.

What's your lead time?

For in-stock products: 3-7 days after payment confirmation. For custom production: 10-15 days. We work with reliable international freight forwarders to ensure on-time delivery worldwide.
