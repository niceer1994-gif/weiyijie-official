Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from our international buyers

What certifications do your plant extracts have?

Our partner factories hold full certifications including GMP, ISO 9001, Kosher, Halal, USDA Organic, EU Organic, HACCP, and FDA registered. We can provide all documentation for your compliance needs.

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

We offer flexible MOQ: we accept small trial orders (1-5kg) for new customers to test quality, and we can handle bulk volume orders for mass production.

Do you provide samples before placing a bulk order?

Yes, we can provide small samples for quality testing. Sample charges can be deducted from your first bulk order.

How do you ensure product quality?

All our plant extracts are tested by third-party labs before shipment. We provide COA (Certificate of Analysis) for every batch. If the product doesn't meet specifications, we accept returns or replacements.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept T/T (bank transfer), L/C, and other common international payment methods for bulk orders.

How long does delivery take?

Usually 7-15 days after payment confirmation, depending on order quantity and product specifications. We work with reliable international freight forwarders to ensure on-time delivery.
